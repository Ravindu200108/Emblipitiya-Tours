Welcome to our website where you will find a selection of itineraries for our wildlife tours and birdwatching in Sri Lanka for 2013. We have been organizing events for nature lovers since 1980 and during the last 33 years over 30,000 tours have been successfully completed. We are also cooperating with many well known wildlife tours with night camping tours and are actively involved in nature conservation. Besides wildlife tours, we offer birdwatching and many natural history, large mammal, culture & nature and wildlife photo tours.
